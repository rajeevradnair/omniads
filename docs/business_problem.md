# Business Problem

Live sports and OTT streaming platforms need to deliver personalized ads during playback without disrupting the viewer experience.

During an ad break, the platform must decide which ad to show based on viewer context, content, placement, campaign eligibility, frequency caps, budget pacing, relevance, and auction ranking.

The hard part is not returning any ad. The hard part is making a fast, traceable, explainable, and scalable decision.

OmniAds models this problem as an AWS-native production-simulation ad decisioning platform.
